Quick Start Guide

  1. Go to Settings → User Info and enter your name, age, retirement age, and annual salary.
  2. Go to Budget and enter your income and planned expenses for this month.
  3. Go to Cash and add your bank accounts with current balances.
  4. Go to Debt and add any loans (student loans, car, etc.).
  5. Go to Goals and add your savings goals (house, vacation, etc.).
  6. Your Dashboard auto-calculates net worth, savings rate, and health score.
  7. At month end, click Close Month to lock data and advance to next month.
  8. Export your data monthly from Settings → Data as a backup.

Key Terms

Net Worth — Total assets minus total debt.
Savings Rate — % of income not spent. Target: 20–30%.
DTI — Debt-to-Income ratio. Monthly debt payments ÷ gross income. Target: <36%.
Emergency Fund — 3–6 months of expenses in liquid cash.
4% Rule — Annual retirement spend = 4% of portfolio. Guides retirement savings targets.
